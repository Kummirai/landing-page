Our breeds

The Labrador Retriever is the most popular dog breed in the world. They are known for being friendly, intelligent, and easy to train. Labradors are often used as working dogs, such as guide dogs and search and rescue dogs.

The Golden Retriever is another popular dog breed. They are similar to Labradors in many ways, but they are generally larger and have a thicker coat of fur. Golden Retrievers are also known for being friendly and gentle.

The German Shepherd is a large, intelligent breed of dog that is often used as a working dog. They are known for their loyalty and obedience and can be trained to perform tasks, such as search and rescue, police work, and assisting people with disabilities.

The Boxer is a medium-sized, short-haired breed of dog that is known for its distinctive "boxer's nose." Boxers are known for being friendly and playful.
